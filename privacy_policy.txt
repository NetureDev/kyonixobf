
<h1>Privacy Policy for LuaObfuscator.com</h1>
<p>
At LuaObfuscator.com, we are committed to protecting the privacy of our users. This privacy policy outlines the information we collect, how we use it, and how we protect your information.
</p>
<h2>Information we collect</h2>
<p>
When you use our service, we collect information about how many Lua script files are uploaded and how many times they are obfuscated. We do not collect or permanently store the contents of your Lua files on our servers. The Lua script files are only kept in RAM for a short duration while you are applying obfuscation. Additionally, we collect information about your browser, device, and IP address. We also use cookies and third-party services to collect information about your use of our platform.
</p>
<h2>Third-party services</h2>
<p>
We use CloudFlare for load balancing and Google Ads for showing third-party ads. Please note that we have no control over the content or policies of these third-party services. You should review their privacy policies to understand how they collect and use your information.
</p>
<h2>Information retention</h2>
<p>
We retain information about the number of files uploaded and obfuscation passes applied for the lifespan of LuaObfuscator.com. This information is used to display statistics on our website and to improve our service.
</p>
<h2>Updates to this privacy policy</h2>
<p>
We may update this privacy policy from time to time to reflect changes in our practices or applicable laws. We encourage you to review this policy regularly to stay informed about how we collect, use, and protect your information.
</p>
